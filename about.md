# Custom Transitions
by [undefined0](user:13351341)

---

Use custom images (or the ones the mod provides) to fade between your scenes, instead of the fade being all at once!
Set the image in the mod settings to an image on your device (or use one of the ones built in to the mod) to get started.

(you can also customise the length of the transition)

Greyscale images in PNG format are recommended, images will be stretched to fit your screen's aspect ratio.



Built-in image credits:
- feather.png by hatmix at https://opengameart.org/content/feather-grayscale-transition-image
- bubbles.png, curtain.png, heart.png, lock.png, saw.png, spiky.png and stars.png from Celeste (would recommend setting edge size to 0!)
- wibblywobbly.png and pixelhash.png from Soulcreek
- All other images except for sog.png created by me
